MLB wild card: Royce Lewis ends Twins’ 18-game playoff skid; Rays’ record-low fans

Downey native Evan Longoria’s glove, JSerra product Royce Lewis’ bat, Trea Turner’s legs and Corey Seager’s answer highlighted the MLB wild-card Game 1s.

Third baseman Evan Longoria won Gold Gloves a long time ago, in 2009 and 2010, his second and third seasons in the big leagues. He won a third in 2017, his last of 10 with the Tampa Bay Rays. And the former Long Beach State, Rio Hondo College and Bellflower St. John Bosco High product made a Gold Glove play Tuesday in the No. 6 seed Arizona Diamondbacks’ 6-3 win over the No. 3 seed Milwaukee Brewers. The winner of the series plays the Dodgers in the National League Division Series beginning Saturday.
